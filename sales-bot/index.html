What it does

Built for businesses that lose buyers inside chat.

Instead of only collecting messages, the bot guides each buyer through the sales conversation and prepares the owner to close with context.

01

Answers sales questions

Handles pricing, service, booking, location, payment, and objection replies from a configurable business profile.

02

Qualifies buyers

Captures what the customer needs, when they need it, budget, contact details, and readiness to proceed.

03

Gives owner control

Saves leads locally, exports CSV files, and generates reusable sales scripts for WhatsApp or manual follow-up.

Sales workflow

Simple enough to sell now. Structured enough to scale later.

The first version works as a website tool. Later it can connect to WhatsApp, Instagram, CRMs, payment links, and real AI APIs once the offer is validated with paying clients.

1

Set up the business offer, prices, FAQs, objections, and payment methods.

2

Let customers chat with the bot and answer the key sales qualification questions.

3

Review hot leads, save notes, export CSV, and follow up with serious buyers.
